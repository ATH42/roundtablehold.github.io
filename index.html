Welcome to the Roundtable Hold

The comprehensive tracker for Elden Ring, made by completionists, for completionists.

This site is still a work in-progress. We are working on it every day.

I have feedback, how can I contribute?

Contributing is easy! And does not require you to know how to code. You can find instructions on the GitHub repository. You can also simply report issues and we'll fix them.

Or you can join the development discord, and ask us there.

Can I use this for multiple characters?

Yup, use the profile selector and buttons in the options tab at the top of the page to setup multiple profiles.

How does the checklist status get saved?

The checklist is saved to your browser's local storage. Be careful when clearing your browser's cache as it will also destroy your saved progress.
